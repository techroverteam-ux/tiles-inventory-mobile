## Key UI/UX Principles

1. **Zoho Inventory Inspired**: Clean, professional design with high information density
2. **Consistent Branding**: Company colors and logo throughout
3. **Material Design 3**: Modern Android design language
4. **Skeleton Loading**: No full-screen spinners, content-aware loading
5. **Button-Level Loading**: Individual button loading states
6. **Responsive Design**: Adapts to different screen sizes
7. **Accessibility**: Proper contrast, touch targets, and screen reader support
8. **Intuitive Navigation**: Clear hierarchy and easy access to main features
